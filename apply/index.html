Attendees of 2017 CUWiP@UCLA.

Interested in Applying?

We are delighted that you are interested in joining us at CUWiP! During the application period (September 1st to October 13th), you must apply directly through the APS CUWiP application site.

Can I apply?

Bear in mind that CUWiP@SoCal is intended to serve applicants from California (south of San Jose), Alaska, and Hawaii. Students from other locations are encouraged to check the map below and apply to the conference site geographically closest to them. While the conference targets undergraduate women and underrepresented genders passionate about physics and astronomy, anyone seriously interested in attending may apply. For other questions about applicant eligibility, visit our FAQ.

the map to view it full-sized.

What will it cost?

There is no cost to apply to CUWiP. If you are admitted, there is a one-time registration fee of $45. While you are expected to make your own travel arrangements, lodging, food, and transportation to conference events will be provided to all participants at no cost. Visit our FAQ for more information about costs and funding.

Will I be admitted?

We anticipate that CUWiP@HMC+POM+CPP will be oversubscribed. Therefore, it is crucial to complete all parts of the application and to write a thoughtful Applicant Statement that conveys how this conference is meaningful for your personal and professional growth. In the event that the conference is oversubscribed, priority will be given to applicants who:

  • Are undergraduate physics, astronomy, or engineering physics majors
  • Are community college students planning to transfer to a physics program at a four year institution
  • Have never attended APS CUWiP
  • Are applying to the conference that is geographically closest to their expected location January 12th to 14th
