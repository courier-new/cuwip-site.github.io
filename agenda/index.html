CUWiP 2018 Agenda

CUWiP 2018 @ HMC + POM + CPP will be spread across the three colleges of Harvey Mudd, Pomona, and Cal Poly Pomona. Attendees will spend one day at each institution, beginning with Friday at Harvey Mudd, Saturday at Pomona, and finishing up on Sunday at Cal Poly Pomona. Keep in mind that this schedule is still incomplete; check back soon for updates!

All events are scheduled in Pacific Standard Time, the local timezone.
